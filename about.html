Mr. Ajumobi Abayomi is a seasoned logistics expert with more than 15 years of experience across international shipping, procurement, export cargo operations and supply-chain strategy. A graduate of LAUTECH, Ogbomoso and Associate Member of the Chartered Institute of Logistics & Supply Chain Management (CILSCM), he leads SwiftSpeed’s global expansion drive.

Building on this strong foundation, Mr. Ajumobi has established himself as a respected leader in the logistics and trade facilitation sector, known for delivering compliant, efficient, and scalable cargo solutions across international corridors.

As Founder and Group Chairman of SwiftSpeed Services, he has overseen the company’s evolution into a trusted logistics partner serving Africa and global markets.

His leadership philosophy centers on integrity, customer satisfaction, innovation, and building sustainable logistics infrastructure that connects African businesses to the global economy.
